A B O U T

Habit Hatcher is a proof-of-concept gamified habit tracker, where completing your goals in turn hatches random birds to collect!

Design studies have shown that people are drawn to random chance games (ex. slots) and also collecting games (ex. Pokemon Go, Cat Collector,etc). Habit Hatcher aims to draw upon these concepts to make completing your goals and turning them into habits something to look forward to.
